The Process

To build this Encryptor we were required to first call the boxes in html and then add JavaScript to make it functional. First, I made the encryption math for just a single letter encryption. It needed to get the letter that was input and convert it one letter forward to the alphabet. Later on, the numbers increased. To jump over the x, y, z letters I needed to reverse the calculation and backtrack the letters instead. Then the decryption method used the reverse way of the encryption to decrypt the message and display it.
